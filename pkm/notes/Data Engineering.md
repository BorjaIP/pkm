**Data Engineering** is the discipline of designing, building, and maintaining the infrastructure and systems that collect, store, transform, and deliver data reliably at scale. It bridges raw data sources and the consumers of that data — including [[Data Applications|data applications]], [[Machine Learning]] models, and [[Business Intelligence]] dashboards.

Data engineers are responsible for building [[Data Orchestration|data pipelines]] (ETL/ELT), managing [[Databases|databases]] and data lakes, ensuring [[Data Governance|data quality and governance]], and enabling efficient [[Data Extraction|data extraction]] from heterogeneous sources. They work closely with [[Data Platforms]] and streaming systems like [[Kafka]] to move and process data at scale.
