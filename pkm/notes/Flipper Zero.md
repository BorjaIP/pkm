**Flipper Zero** is a **portable, multi-tool device for interacting with electronic systems**, mainly used by **security researchers, hackers (ethical), makers, and IT learners**. It looks like a small gadget with a screen and buttons, but inside it combines many wireless and hardware interfaces into one pocket-sized device.
